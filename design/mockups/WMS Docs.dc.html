Q3 Finance — Operating Plan

Overview

This document outlines the operating plan for Q3 2026. Revenue target is $240k, with a total budgeted spend of $71,840 across all categories. The tax reserve allocation remains fixed at $62,000 per quarter.

Revenue

The primary revenue stream remains retainer contracts with Acme Corp and Halcyon Labs, totalling $175k/quarter. New pipeline from Beta Corp ($65k) is expected to close in early Q3.

Expenses

All categories are within budget with the exception of contractors, which ran 4.8% over in June due to scope expansion on the WMS build. We will re-baseline the contractor budget to $28k/month for Q3.
